Bell Cranel (ベル・クラネル)

A boy who wants to be a hero

About Bell Cranel's life:

  • Bell was the offspring of Meteria, member of Hera Familia
  • Raised in a rural village by Zeus, his grandfather and member of Zeus Familia
  • Almost killed by a goblin but was saved by his grandfather
  • Read about heroes and dreamed of becoming one
  • Decided to live the village and travelled Orario tofufill his dreams
  • When he arrived in the city, he tried to enter from different Familias but was rejected. By coincidence encounter, he met a goddess named, Hestia, who offered him to become the first member of Hestia Familia.
  • He became the first member of Hestia Familia
  • Achieved level 2 in one and a half month, level 3 in one month and level 4 in two months
  • He gained a new title "Little Rookie"
  • He defeated the "Black" Goliath inside the dungeon and saved his allies
  • When they were exploring the new dungeon that appeared in the city, he encountered and saved a wounded monster with rational thinking.
  • He protected the Xenos, a group of monsters capable of thinking against adventurers.
  • Had a duel with the "Black Minotaur" which led to a draw result of the battle
  • Gained a new title "Rabbit Foot"
  • Defeated the dungeon boss "Green Moss"
